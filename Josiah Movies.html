3 Movies I have seen this year, ranked!

3 movies I have seen in the year 2023 and I rank them.

Super Mario Brothers

I actually went to the theater to watch this movie. I took my son as well and this was the first movie he went to in the theater. It is everything you would expect in a movie based on Mario. Pacing is a little problem. but still a good movie

Major League

An older movie, but still a classic. There are parts in it that is of the times. But also, really funny

Dungeons and Dragons: Honor Among Thieves

A fun movie. Biggest problem is pacing as it blazes through the story. But there were funny parts in the movie and I would recommend to see at least once.
